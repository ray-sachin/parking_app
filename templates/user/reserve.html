Reservation Information
How it works
  1. Select a parking lot from the dropdown
  2. Enter your vehicle number
  3. Submit the form to reserve a spot
  4. A spot will be automatically assigned to you
  5. Use the release button when you leave
Pricing

You will be charged based on the hourly rate of the parking lot and the actual time your vehicle is parked.

Important Notes
  • You can only have one active reservation at a time
  • Make sure to release your spot when you leave
  • Payment is calculated when you release the spot
